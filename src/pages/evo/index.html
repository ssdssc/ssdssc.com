Evolvion Event map

With Four Stages

Navigate Evolvion ’25 with ease — your map to the ultimate tech-powered quiz showdown, from first buzz to grand finale.

PHASE 01

Moment | Online Quiz Round

Phase One of Evolvian kicks off with Moment — a fully online inter-school battle of intellect. Over 30 schools connect through SSDSSC’s custom-built quiz platform, competing live in real time to secure a place among the Top 10 teams.

powerd with quiz app

50+ teams

PHASE 02

Knockouts | On-Site Quiz Showdown

Held at our school premises, this on-ground round features the Top 10 teams from Moment, battling head-to-head in a high-stakes tech-powered setup. Each team is equipped with an interconnected device linked to the Evolvian network, with real-time scores flashing on video walls. Only the Top 4 will advance.

Rapid app

PHASE 03

Hazard | Semi-Finals

The tension rises in Hazzard, where 4 teams face off in intense one-on-one matchups. This round introduces interactive lifelines like Ask the Audience and 50:50, with the audience plugged into the Evolvian network and influencing the outcome in real time. Only 2 teams survive.

EVO GRND | Grand Finale
